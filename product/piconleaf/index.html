Description

Description

 

 

What else can you give for anniversaries and co? The photo books are already piling up, and they also use up tons of paper! How about something a little out of the ordinary?

This is where our engraved sheet comes into play. Yes, you heard us right, with our high-tech machine we can now engrave photos on a real leaf from a tree! The whole thing is then lovingly packed into a picture frame, which is available in three colours to fit into any household.

How does the whole thing work?

  1. You upload a photo when you place your order. Please read the photo guidelines below.
  2. Select a colour tone for the picture frame
  3. Order securely via Paypal, Klarna or credit card in our shop, 100% secured by Stripe
  4. We engrave your photo on the leaf and it will be with you in 10-14 days!

What makes a perfect photo?

  • The subject must be recognisable with strong contours. Portraits in good light are ideal for this
  • 1 – 2 persons can be pictured, unfortunately no group pictures
  • High contrast with ideally a neutral background
  • Unfortunately, pictures with a lot of hair are not good, i.e. no photos of pets & co.

If you have any questions, please contact us on the contact page!

 

This is how you order:

  1. Upload your desired image. If you place more than one order, add several at once.
  2. Choose the right frame
  3. Add the item to the shopping cart
  4. Go to the checkout and pay securely
